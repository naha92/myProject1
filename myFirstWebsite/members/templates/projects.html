Projects

Project 1

Project 1 focuses on designing and developing a website named "Code Crusaders" to showcase the skills of fellow students in DevSecOps, available for freelance projects. The website utilizes Django for backend development, Bootstrap 5 for frontend, and is deployed on AWS using Terraform. It incorporates a lint error checker on GitHub, requiring approvals for changes. The project addresses UI/UX design, GDPR compliance, data breach prevention, and automatic deployment.

Project 2

Project 2 involves collaborative work within a team, building upon the foundation laid in Project 1. The objective is to showcase a DevOps culture and embrace agile working methodologies. The team, comprised of Thasbia Sultana, Hayden Buckley-Smith, Devina Moore, Cara Swift, and Nathan Halter, will collectively enhance the "Code Crusaders" website. Each team member will contribute their specialized skills, fostering collaboration and efficient communication. DevOps practices will be implemented, emphasizing CI/CD, automation, and iterative development. Agile methodologies will be employed to ensure adaptability to changing project requirements, short feedback loops, and regular team collaboration.
